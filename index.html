Valcaron

Advanced aerial systems for rapid situational awareness

What we do

Valcaron develops advanced aerial systems that help emergency, security, and defence teams gain rapid situational awareness in challenging environments. Our technology supports faster and safer decision-making during critical operations by improving the speed and accuracy of locating people, hazards, and key points of interest when time and clarity are essential.

News & Updates

13 January 2026

Accepted into Techscaler

Valcaron was accepted into the Techscaler programme, providing access to structured support for early-stage company formation, expert guidance, and a national community of founders.

The programme supports the exploration and validation of Valcaron's technical and commercial direction, helping to shape the transition from early research toward a sustainable company.

December 2025

Runner-up in Global Innovation Challenge

Valcaron was selected as runner-up in a global accelerator programme, receiving funding to support the purchase of specialist hardware and continued technical development.

The award recognises the potential impact of Valcaron's work in improving situational awareness for search & rescue, security, and defence applications, and supports the transition from early research toward deployable field systems.

August 2025

Valcaron joins the Heriot-Watt EBS Incubator

Valcaron was accepted into the Heriot-Watt University EBS Incubator in August 2025. The year-long programme supports early-stage technology companies through structured mentoring, technical development support, and access to academic and industry networks.

Participation in the incubator enables Valcaron to further develop its research-driven approach to onboard aerial intelligence while engaging closely with experienced founders, engineers, and domain specialists.
